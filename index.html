About

I'm Will Propp. I'm a recent graduate of the University of California, Santa Cruz, where I received a BA in Art & Design: Games & Playable Media. I love making games, websites and sometimes trying to make web apps. Crafting flexible solutions to problems through programming is one of the best feelings in the world to me. I'm obsessed with making things, whether its games, music, animations, or graphic design projects, there's nothing I love more than learning about something new and using that newfound knowledge to create something. Outside of school and work, I enjoy making music, spending time with friends and family, and playing games.
